About

We believe that independent bookstores play a critical role in their communities. When you shop at an independently-owned business, you keep the money you spend in your own community. Buying local also means less packaging, less fuel for transportation and a smaller carbon footprint. As a reader, your actions are part of a larger picture. We're all about helping you find independent booksellers near you so you can feel good about the books you purchase.
